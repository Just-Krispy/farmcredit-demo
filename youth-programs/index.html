Three Rivers College Partnership

Farm Credit SEMO and Three Rivers College announced a historic 12-year partnership in September 2024 to build agriculture education classrooms across Southeast Missouri. The Farm Credit Ag Classroom at the Three Rivers Farm in Fairdealing, Missouri, serves FFA chapters, 4-H clubs, and agribusiness training programs.
